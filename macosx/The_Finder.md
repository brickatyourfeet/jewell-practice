# The Finder

### !challenge
* type: paragraph

* title: Question 1

##### !question
What is the Finder?
##### !end-question

##### !explanation
It's how your files are organized on a Mac. It's equivalent to Window Explorer, or My Computer, on Windows.
##### !end-explanation

##### !end-question
